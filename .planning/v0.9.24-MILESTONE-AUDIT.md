## Remaining Tech Debt (2 items, non-blocking)

1. **CostTracker costLimit ordering (MEDIUM):** CostTracker instantiated in `hydrateAgentRunState` before `loadSessionConfig` sets `session.safetyConfig`. CostTracker.costLimit always 2.00 regardless of mode. The fallback `checkSafetyBreakers` path reads `session.safetyConfig.costLimit` directly and works correctly, so budget enforcement is not broken -- only the CostTracker instance has the wrong limit. Fix: reorder `hydrateAgentRunState` after `loadSessionConfig`.

2. **Unused emitter in options (LOW):** `emitter` key passed to `runAgentLoop` options but never destructured. Emitter is captured in hook closures. No functional impact.
