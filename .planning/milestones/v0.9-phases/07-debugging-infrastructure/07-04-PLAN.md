The existing click handler (line 4087) demonstrates the required pattern:

1. Capture startTime at function start: `const startTime = Date.now();`
2. On success: call actionRecorder.record() with full context including elementDetails, coordinatesUsed, success: true, hadEffect: true, duration
3. On failure: call actionRecorder.record() with diagnostic from generateDiagnostic()

Example from click handler success path:
```javascript

  selectorTried,
  selectorUsed: selectorTried,
  elementFound: true,
  elementDetails: captureElementDetails(element),
  coordinatesUsed,
  coordinateSource,
  success: true,
  hadEffect: true,
  duration: Date.now() - startTime
});
```

```javascript

  success: false,
  error: 'Element not found and no coordinates available for fallback',

  duration: Date.now() - startTime
});
```
