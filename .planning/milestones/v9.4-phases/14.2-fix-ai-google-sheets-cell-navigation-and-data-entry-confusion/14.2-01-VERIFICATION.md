### Gaps Summary

No gaps found. All four must-have truths are verified against the actual codebase:

1. The site guide (`google-sheets.js`) now has a CRITICAL WARNING block at the very top of its guidance text, explicitly separating the navigate step (Name Box target) from the data entry step (active cell). This appears before the CANVAS-BASED GRID section for maximum visibility.

2. The multitab TASK_PROMPTS "WRITING TO GOOGLE SHEETS" section was expanded from 3 lines to 12 lines with a 5-step numbered procedure and an explicit NEVER warning against typing cell references as data values.

3. The `buildMinimalUpdate` method injects a 4-line "GOOGLE SHEETS REMINDER" block on every continuation iteration when the URL matches a Google Sheets document pattern (`/d/` path segment required). The `MINIMAL_CONTINUATION_PROMPT` constant is unchanged.

4. The `fillSheetData` career workflow path is intact. The `GOOGLE SHEETS DATA ENTRY SESSION` directive, `sheetsData` injection logic, and all 7 `fillSheetData` references in ai-integration.js are unmodified.

Both commits (`be1d760` and `8f2e6d5`) are verified in git log and their stat output confirms only the two target files were modified.
