**Per-locale checks (~20 strings spanning shell/home/about/agents/privacy/support namespaces):**
1. **Brand tokens preserved verbatim** -- grep targets for `FSB`, `MCP`, `Claude`, `Codex`, `Cursor`, `OpenClaw`, `ClawHub` literal occurrences; ensure none translated.

3. **Tone register sanity** -- es LATAM neutral (no peninsular "vosotros"), de formal Sie throughout, ja です/ます endings, zh-CN simplified mainland tech vocab, zh-TW Taiwan tech vocab (`軟體` not `軟件`, `網路` not `網絡`, `伺服器` not `服務器`).
4. **No obviously machine-mangled output** -- empty or null-like targets, unescaped `<` `>`, broken `&apos;`/`&quot;`.

**Verification:** Findings table per locale in SUMMARY. Acceptance:
