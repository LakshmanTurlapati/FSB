<objective>
Create a cookie-opt-out.js site guide with a rejectAllCookies workflow and DARK-02 guidance for navigating EU news site cookie consent banners that hide the reject/decline option behind a secondary "Manage preferences" layer.

Purpose: DARK-02 requires opting out of all non-essential cookies on an EU news site where the reject option is hidden. This is a "Visual Ambiguity & Dark Patterns" edge case -- cookie consent banners (OneTrust, Quantcast, Cookiebot, TrustArc) make "Accept All" visually prominent (large, colored button) while hiding "Reject All" behind a "Manage preferences" or "Cookie settings" intermediary screen. Some sites have no "Reject All" button at all and require toggling individual cookie categories off then clicking "Save preferences." The AI must: (a) detect which CMP platform is in use via DOM selectors, (b) locate the "Manage preferences" button instead of clicking "Accept All," (c) navigate the secondary preferences layer, (d) find and click "Reject All" or toggle all categories off and save, (e) verify cookies were declined. The site guide must document: target EU news sites, CMP platform detection, the hidden reject path for each platform, and the full opt-out workflow.

Output: site-guides/utilities/cookie-opt-out.js and background.js updated with importScripts entry
