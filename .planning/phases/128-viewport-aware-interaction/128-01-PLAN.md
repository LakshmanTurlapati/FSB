<objective>
Make click and hover work on any element regardless of scroll position by enhancing the scroll-into-view pipeline to account for fixed/sticky headers and verify actual visibility via elementFromPoint before interaction.

Purpose: Off-viewport elements currently fail to click when fixed headers (common on most websites) obscure them after scrollIntoView. The AI sees the element in the DOM but cannot interact with it -- a "readable but not clickable" gap that causes automation failures.

Output: Enhanced content/accessibility.js with header-aware scrolling and post-scroll visibility verification.
</objective>
